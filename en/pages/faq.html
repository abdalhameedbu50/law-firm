Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our legal services

What legal services does Al Hassan Law Firm provide?

Al Hassan Law Firm offers a wide range of legal services including legal consultations, litigation and disputes, bankruptcy requests, contract drafting and review, intellectual property registration, foreign investment and company establishment, legal management for enterprises, debt collection, franchise agreements, and documentation services. You can find detailed information about each service on our Services page.

How can I book a legal consultation with one of your lawyers?

You can book a legal consultation through several methods: via our website on the "Book Legal Consultation" page, by calling directly at +966 50 316 9240, or by sending an email to Info@alhassanlawfirm.com. We will contact you to schedule your consultation, whether in person at our office or remotely via video conferencing.

What is the cost of a legal consultation?

The cost of a legal consultation varies depending on the type, nature, and duration of the consultation. Initial basic consultations may be free in some cases, while specialized and detailed consultations range from 500 to 1500 Saudi Riyals. You can inquire about the specific cost when booking your consultation. We also offer monthly consultation packages for companies and institutions at competitive prices.

What documents are required to file a lawsuit?

The required documents to file a lawsuit vary depending on the type and subject of the case, but generally include: national ID, residency permit, or commercial registration for the plaintiff, documents supporting the claim (contracts, correspondence, receipts, reports, photos, etc.), a notarized power of attorney (if appointing a lawyer), and any other relevant documents related to the case. Our lawyer will accurately determine the required documents after reviewing your case.

Does the firm provide legal services to individuals or only to companies?

Al Hassan Law Firm provides legal services to both individuals and companies/institutions. We handle individual cases in various fields such as personal status, labor, real estate, and commercial matters, as well as specialized services for companies and institutions in areas of investment, company formation, commercial contracts, intellectual property protection, and more.

How are legal fees calculated for cases?

Legal fees for cases are calculated in several ways depending on the nature, complexity, and expected duration of the case. Common methods include: fixed fees (a set amount for the entire case), hourly rates (a price per hour of legal work), or a percentage of the claim value (especially in collection and financial claims). In all cases, fees are clearly and in writing agreed upon before starting work on the case.

Can I get a legal consultation remotely?

Yes, Al Hassan Law Firm offers remote legal consultations via video conferencing tools such as Zoom or Microsoft Teams, or by phone. This service is available to clients inside and outside the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, providing flexibility and convenience while maintaining the quality of legal advice. You can book a remote consultation through our website or by contacting the office directly.

What is the expected duration of litigation?

The duration of litigation varies depending on the type and complexity of the case and the competent court. Generally, simple cases may take 3 to 6 months, while complex cases may last a year or more, especially if the judgment is appealed. At Al Hassan Law Firm, we always strive to expedite litigation procedures as much as possible while maintaining the quality of legal representation, and we provide clients with a realistic estimate of the expected duration based on our experience with similar cases.

Does the firm offer trademark registration services?

Yes, Al Hassan Law Firm provides trademark registration and protection services in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Gulf countries, Arab countries, and internationally. We are accredited agents of the Saudi Authority for Intellectual Property and offer comprehensive services including trademark search, application preparation, procedure follow-up with competent authorities, responding to objections, trademark renewal, and monitoring trademark infringement cases.

What legal management services for enterprises does the firm offer?

The legal management services for enterprises offered by Al Hassan Law Firm include: reviewing and drafting contracts and agreements, providing daily legal consultations, preparing and updating internal policies and regulations, representing the enterprise in legal disputes, dealing with government and regulatory authorities, protecting intellectual property rights, assisting with regulatory compliance, and providing legal training for employees. We offer these services through annual or monthly contracts according to the needs of the enterprise.

Are there fees for attending court sessions?

Court attendance fees are usually included in the agreed-upon legal fees in the power of attorney contract. In some cases, especially if the court is in a city other than the office's location, additional fees may be charged to cover travel and accommodation costs. All these details are clarified in the power of attorney contract before starting work on the case to ensure full transparency.

Can I get a free legal consultation?

We offer in some cases free initial consultations that are brief and intended to evaluate the case and determine the initial steps. These free consultations are limited in scope and duration and aim to help the client understand their legal situation in general. For a detailed and comprehensive legal consultation, consultation fees are determined based on its nature and duration. We also offer pro bono legal services in some humanitarian and charitable cases as part of our social responsibility.
