Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our legal services

What legal services does Al-Hassan Law Firm provide?

Al-Hassan Law Firm provides a wide range of legal services including legal consultations, litigation and disputes, bankruptcy applications, contract drafting and review, intellectual property rights registration, foreign investment and company establishment, legal management for enterprises, debt collection, franchise agreements, and documentation services. You can find details of each service on our Services page.

How can I book a legal consultation with one of your lawyers?

You can book a legal consultation through several methods: via our website through the "Book a Legal Consultation" page, by directly calling +966 50 316 9240, or by sending an email to Info@alhassanlawfirm.com. We will contact you to schedule the consultation, which can be in-person at our office or remotely via video conferencing.

What is the cost of a legal consultation?

The cost of a legal consultation varies depending on the type, nature, and duration of the consultation. Initial basic consultations may be free in some cases, while specialized and detailed consultations range from 500 to 1,500 Saudi Riyals. You can inquire about the specific cost when booking the consultation. We also offer monthly consultation packages for companies and institutions at competitive rates.

What documents are required to file a lawsuit?

The documents required to file a lawsuit vary depending on the type and subject of the case, but generally include: national ID, residence permit, or commercial registration of the plaintiff; supporting documents for the case (contracts, correspondence, receipts, reports, photos, etc.); notarized power of attorney (if appointing a lawyer); and any other documents relevant to the case. Our lawyers will specify the exact documents needed after studying your case.

Does the firm provide legal services to individuals or only to companies?

Al-Hassan Law Firm provides legal services to both individuals and companies/institutions alike. We handle individual cases in various fields such as personal status, labor, real estate, and commercial cases, and we also provide specialized services to companies and institutions in areas of investment, company establishment, commercial contracts, intellectual property protection, and more.

How are attorney fees calculated for cases?

Attorney fees for cases are calculated in several ways depending on the nature, complexity, and expected duration of the case. Common methods include: fixed fees (a specific amount for the entire case), hourly fees (setting a price for each hour of legal work), or a percentage of the claim value (especially in collection and financial claims cases). In all cases, fees are clearly agreed upon in writing before starting work on the case.

Can I get a remote legal consultation?

Yes, Al-Hassan Law Firm offers remote legal consultation services via video conferencing platforms such as Zoom or Microsoft Teams, or by phone. This service is available to clients inside and outside Saudi Arabia, providing flexibility and convenience while maintaining the quality of legal consultation. You can book a remote consultation through our website or by directly contacting the office.

What is the expected duration of litigation for cases?

The duration of litigation varies depending on the type of case, its complexity, and the competent court. Generally, simple cases may take 3 to 6 months, while complex cases may continue for a year or more, especially if the judgment is appealed. At Al-Hassan Law Firm, we always strive to expedite litigation procedures as much as possible while maintaining the quality of legal representation, and we provide clients with a realistic estimate of the expected duration based on our experience in similar cases.

Does the firm provide trademark registration services?

Yes, Al-Hassan Law Firm provides trademark registration and protection services in Saudi Arabia, Gulf countries, Arab countries, and internationally. We are authorized agents of the Saudi Authority for Intellectual Property, and we provide comprehensive services including searching for similar trademarks, preparing registration applications, following up on procedures with the competent authorities, responding to objections, renewing trademarks, and following up on trademark infringement cases.

What legal management services for enterprises does the firm offer?

The legal management services for enterprises offered by Al-Hassan Law Firm include: reviewing and drafting contracts and agreements, providing daily legal consultations, preparing and updating internal regulations and policies, representing the enterprise in legal disputes, dealing with governmental and regulatory bodies, protecting intellectual property rights, assisting in compliance with regulations and laws, and providing legal training for employees. We offer these services through annual or monthly contracts according to the needs of the enterprise.

Are there fees for attending court sessions?

Usually, the fees for attending court sessions are included within the attorney fees agreed upon in the power of attorney contract. In some cases, especially if the court is in a city other than the office location, there may be additional fees to cover travel and accommodation costs. All these details are clarified in the power of attorney contract before starting work on the case to ensure complete transparency.

Can I get a free legal consultation?

In some cases, we provide free initial and brief legal consultations to assess the case and determine the initial steps. These free consultations are limited in scope and duration, and aim to help the client understand their legal situation in general. For a detailed and comprehensive legal consultation, consultation fees are determined according to its nature and duration. We also provide free legal services (pro bono) in some humanitarian and charitable cases as part of our social responsibility.
