Frequently Asked Questions About Legal Consultations

Answers to common questions about our legal consultation service

How long is a legal consultation?

A legal consultation typically lasts between 30 to 60 minutes, depending on the nature and complexity of the consultation.

How are legal consultation fees determined?

Consultation fees are determined based on the type and complexity of the consultation. You will be informed of the required fees after submitting your consultation request.

Can I request a remote legal consultation?

Yes, you can request a remote legal consultation via phone or video conferencing applications such as Zoom or Microsoft Teams.

What documents are required for a legal consultation?

The required documents vary depending on the type of consultation. It is preferable to attach all documents related to the consultation topic to provide better service.

How long does it take to confirm a consultation booking?

Your consultation booking will be confirmed within 24 hours of receiving your request and verifying payment.
